"From an early age, I have been passionate about shapes, mechanisms and electronics. At Ghent University, I am studying industrial design to explore that passion and make it more tangible. Through this webpage, I would like to show a number of completed projects. These projects are based on a thorough theoretical knowledge of mathematics, physics, electricity, materials, thermodynamics and production techniques, among other things."

CAD

Solid and surface modelling, analyses, and creating and interpreting technical drawings. All this with parts and assemblies.

CAE

Analysing forces, stresses, displacement, KNIK and eigenvalues using Siemens NX's solver 101 linear statics, 103 real eigenvalues, 103 response dynamics and 105 Linear Buckling.

Production techniques

PLA and SLS printing, injection moulding, milling, thermoforming, laser cutting... We look at these both theoretically and practically, first drawing the piece in CAD, slicing it and then sending it to the machine.

Graphic design

Rendering, brochures and mock-ups.

Programming

Arduino, Python, HTML, CSS, C# and GUIs. Click on the images to view the projects in detail.
